Sure, here is a simple recipe for making tomato fried eggs:

1. **Ingredients:**
    - 2 tomatoes
    - 3 eggs
    - 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
    - Salt to taste
    - Sugar to taste (optional)
    - Chopped green onions (optional)

2. **Instructions:**
    1. Wash the tomatoes and cut them into small wedges.
    2. Beat the eggs in a bowl and add a pinch of salt.
    3. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
    4. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan and scramble them until they are just set. Remove the eggs from the pan and set aside.
    5. Add another tablespoon of vegetable oil to the pan and heat it up.
    6. Add the tomato wedges to the pan and stir-fry for a few minutes until they start to soften.
    7. Add a pinch of salt and a little sugar (if using) to the tomatoes.
    8. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and mix them with the tomatoes.
    9. Cook for another minute or two until everything is well combined and heated through.
    10. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired and serve hot.

Enjoy your tomato fried eggs!
